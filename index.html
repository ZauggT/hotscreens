twenty-four hours on two screens

The screen is one of the most existential organs of a computer. It reflects the demands and orders of an individual. This site is a record of two users habits, pleasures, obligations and therefore the relationship to their computers.

about

Our Laptops took screenshots of whatever we were doing every 10 minutes for 24 hours. This task was was automated with a Windows app for user1 and an Apple automator Script for user2. The screenshots are squeezed to a line of information. The intent of this project is to show what we are doing on our laptops, and give a glimpse of our daily screen time of working days.
